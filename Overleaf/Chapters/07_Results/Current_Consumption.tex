\subsection{Current Consumption Analysis}
\autoref{fig:cc_full} shows the testresults from a full system implementation, tested in a climate chamber under a temperature range of \SI{-20}{\celsius} to \SI{+50}{\celsius}, with constant humidity. The testresults showed that the MCU has a higher current consumption in EM0 (run mode) at low temperatures and vice versa a higher current consumption in EM4 (sleep mode) at high temperatures.

The measurements fit the datasheet values where \SI{0.17}{\micro\ampere} was the lowest EM4 current consumption and a current consumption between \SI{5.612}{\micro\ampere} and \SI{6.674}{\micro\ampere}
