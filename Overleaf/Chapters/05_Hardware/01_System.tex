\chapter{Hardware} \label{sec:Hardware}

Each face of the wind turbine nut has dimensions of 3 cm × 3 cm, providing surface area for mounting a custom designed printed circuit board (PCB). The PCB will be optimized for low-power operation, high-accuracy analog-to-digital converter (ADC) measurements, reliable wireless communication, and stable performance across varying temperature and humidity conditions. In addition, the design will emphasize cost-effectiveness while maintaining robustness and measurement integrity.

The PCB will be powered by a side-mounted battery, selected to provide long operational lifetime and dependable performance under the environmental conditions encountered in wind turbine applications. The integrated system will enable continuous monitoring and wireless transmission of sensor data while minimizing power consumption and maintenance requirements.
