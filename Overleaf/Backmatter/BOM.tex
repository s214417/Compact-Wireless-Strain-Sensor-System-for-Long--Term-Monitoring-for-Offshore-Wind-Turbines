\chapter{Bill of Material} \label{BOM}
The Bill of Materials is divided into PCB-mounted components and external components, the latter comprising the interconnecting cables, the lithium battery, and the battery holder. Unit prices reflect volume pricing and were sourced from DigiKey in June 2026, with the exception of the battery holder, which was obtained from RS Components (dk.rs), the lithium battery, which was sourced from NKON (nkon.nl), and the MCU EFR32MG22E224F512IM32, which was sourced from Mouser (mouser.dk).
