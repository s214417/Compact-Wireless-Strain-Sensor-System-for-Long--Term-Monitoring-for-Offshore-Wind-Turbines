\textbf{Layout and dimensions}
The strain gauge consists of a strip of monocrystalline silicon with attached gold leads with a diameter of 0.07 mm. The silicon strip is self-supporting, does not require a support pad, which is necessary for wire and foil strain gauges, and is electrically isolated from the measured object with a layer of hardened glue of around 0.03 mm, created before sticking the strain gauge. (silicon deforms without measurable hysteresis up to 300 ºC)
