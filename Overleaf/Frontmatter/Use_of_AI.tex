\section*{Use of Artificial Intelligence}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Use of Artificial Intelligence}

Artificial intelligence (AI) tools have been utilized in the work of this project. Specifically, AI assistance was employed in selected less important coding tasks related to Arduino-based test setup scripts, and MATLAB plotting scripts. Additionally, AI has been used to debugging problems related to microcontroller firmware and wireless communication protocol.
AI has also been used to support the writing process by helping ensure clear and professional language, as the authors are not native English speakers.
The AI models used in this project has been accessed through GitHub Copilot.
