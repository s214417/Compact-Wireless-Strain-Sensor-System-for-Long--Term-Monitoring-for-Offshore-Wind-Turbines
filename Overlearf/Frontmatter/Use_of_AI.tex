\section*{Use of Artificial Intelligence}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Use of Artificial Intelligence}

Artificial intelligence (AI) tools were utilized in this project for selected tasks. Specifically, AI assistance was employed in coding tasks of lesser importance, including Arduino-based test setup scripts and MATLAB plotting scripts. AI was also used to support the development of microcontroller firmware and wireless communication protocols, as well as to optimize the code.
Additionally, AI assisted in the writing process by helping ensure clear and professional language, as the authors are not native English speakers.
The AI models used were drawn from the tools available in GitHub Copilot and Claude.
